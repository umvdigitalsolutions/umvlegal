Copyright Protection Checklist

Use this checklist before discussing copyright registration or protection.

1. Type of work: artistic, literary, musical, cinematographic, software, photograph, design content, or other work
2. Title of the work
3. Name, address, and contact details of the author
4. Name, address, and contact details of the owner/applicant
5. Whether the author and owner are the same person/entity
6. Date and place of creation
7. Date and place of first publication, if published
8. Copies or files of the work in final form
9. No objection certificate or assignment, if the applicant is not the author
10. Employment or commissioning agreement, if the work was created for a business
11. Identity and address proof details of applicant/author
12. Details of any prior publication, licensing, or commercial use

Note: This checklist is for general preparation only and is not legal advice.
