Brand Protection Checklist

Use this checklist when selecting or protecting a business name, logo, or brand.

1. Shortlist the exact brand name, logo, and tagline
2. Search for identical or similar marks before launch
3. Check domain name and social media handle availability
4. Identify all goods and services connected with the brand
5. Select relevant trademark classes for current and future business plans
6. Keep dated proof of first use, including invoices, packaging, website screenshots, and advertisements
7. Avoid descriptive, generic, or commonly used industry terms where possible
8. File trademark applications before major marketing or expansion
9. Monitor similar filings, objections, and market misuse
10. Use proper brand ownership documents for founders, companies, designers, and agencies
11. Protect creative assets with copyright where applicable
12. Plan international filings before entering overseas markets

Note: This checklist is for general preparation only and is not legal advice.
