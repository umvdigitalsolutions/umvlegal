Trademark Filing Checklist

Use this checklist before starting a trademark search or filing discussion.

1. Brand name, logo, tagline, or device mark to be protected
2. Applicant name and type: individual, proprietorship, partnership, LLP, company, trust, or other entity
3. Complete applicant address and contact details
4. Business activity and exact goods or services offered under the mark
5. Proposed trademark class, if already known
6. Date of first use in India, if the mark is already in use
7. Proof of use, such as invoices, website pages, packaging, brochures, social media pages, or advertisements
8. MSME or Startup India certificate, if available
9. Power of attorney or authorization document
10. Clear image file for logo/device marks
11. List of similar names or competitor marks known to you
12. Expansion plans for other products, services, or countries

Note: This checklist is for general preparation only and is not legal advice.
